What is My Senior?

My Senior is a mentorship platform connecting students and alumni to improve communication, get guidance, and make career decisions with the help of experienced seniors.

Mentorship

Verified alumni mentors provide personalized English communication guidance to help you excel.

Discussion

Engage in topic-based group discussions to practice and improve your communication skills.

Growth

Track your progress, complete milestones, and receive certificates for your achievements.

How It Works

Getting started with My Senior is simple and straightforward

1

Sign Up

Choose your role as a Mentor (verified alumni) or Mentee (student) and create your profile.

2

Connect

The admin connects you with suitable mentors/mentees. Each mentor is assigned up to 3 mentees.

3

Learn & Grow

Schedule sessions via Google Meet, access resources, and track your communication progress.

Features

My Senior offers a comprehensive suite of features to support your English communication journey

One-on-One Mentoring

Personalized sessions with verified alumni mentors to improve your English communication skills.

Group Discussion

Topic-based group discussions to practice communication in a collaborative environment.

Subscription Benefits

Premium features including advanced resources, additional sessions, and priority support.

Alumni Network Access

Connect with a wide network of alumni for broader exposure and career opportunities.
